The Diamond

The diamond is the university of sheffield's brand new learning space, having opened in September of this year. The diamond is open 24/7 and offers 1000 study spaces to students from any department.

Alongside quiet study rooms there are also group-study spaces for collaborative work. There are charging points in all of the study area for student laptops and phones, as well as laptops available for loan from the information desk on level 4, and a host of desktop computing facilities throughout the space.

Alongside these private-study spaces the diamond also provides leading edge work areas for various different disciplines from engineering to computer science and biology.
