Hello, I'm Euan Rochester, and welcome to the small part of the web I call my own.

I'm a tech-head from the north of England. While I am a fan of most anything technical I am happiest when elbow deep in code. Having picked up coding as a hobby at a young age I have taught myself several languages to some degree, including C++, Python, Ruby, Java, and Javascript.

I am currently a Computer Science student at the university of sheffield currently in my first year. In my spare time I enjoy reading books (such as "The Necroscope" by Brian Lumley), listening to podcasts (such as "Hello internet" by CGPGrey and Brady Haran), watching youtube videos (either entertaining, or informational) and occasionally doing more active things such as rock climbing or biking.

On my site you'll find news from, and information about, my university degree in Computer Science and my time here at the University of Sheffield.

If you need to get in contact with me you just need to fill in the form on my contact page and I'll try to get back to you as soon as possible.
