Introduction to Software Engineering: COM1001

This module introduces the concepts of software engineering which are needed to develop complex systems which meet certain requirements in a given domain, and involves the whole software engineering process from requirements to implementation and testing.

This module also helps to give experience of working in teams, and at a larger scale than many of the other first year modules, allowing students to become used to team tools such as version control.
